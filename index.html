Bio


Hello!

I'm Christine Ann McDowell, a current 1st year student studying Interaction Design at Ulster university. As a keen designer and developer, I understand the perfect user interface should look good and feel even better. Alongside my clients, I uncover problems and solve them. In short, I create bolder online experiences.


Principles

UX is the sum of all things. There’s no style over substance here. I bring together form and the function to create something that looks good and performs brilliantly. Simple.

Perfection doesn’t do a 9-5. And neither do I.

I’m not bound to any time zones or tied to a desk. The project dictates where I need to be and who I need to collaborate with. Essentially, I always go the extra mile

Clients. Curated.

I take on a limited number of projects at one time. This quality control means I can give each one exactly what it needs – focus and time.

Creator. Partner. Collaborator.

Ideas are far from linear. As designer and developer, I can spearhead, support or polish your project or product at any stage it needs it.

Clean – In thinking, in style.

Simple, minimal and effective. This is my mantra and method. Clean code, fuss-free interfaces and expert execution, design as it should be.
